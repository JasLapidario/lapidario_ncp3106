Hi, I'm JASMINE

Jasmine Sophia Lapidario was born in Quezon City and grew up in Mandaluyong City, where she currently resides. She is a dedicated student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the University of the East in Manila.

In 2019, during the pandemic, she paused her studies and entered the BPO industry, where she gained valuable experience as a Customer Service Representative over two years. In this role, she specialized in Customer Service, Chat Support, and Tech Support, consistently achieving top performance and earning high KPI and QA scores.

Academically, Jasmine was part of the first batch of K-12 Senior High School students, where she studied Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) at Jose Rizal University. She later pursued a Bachelor of Science in Architecture at Adamson University. She has since transitioned to studying Computer Engineering at the University of the East, developing a strong foundation in programming languages such as C++, Java, and Python, as well as skills in circuit design and analysis, embedded systems, network security, software development, problem-solving, and technical support and troubleshooting.

Outside of her academic and professional pursuits, Jasmine is passionate about traveling and enjoys riding her motorcycle. These hobbies reflect her adventurous spirit and desire to explore new places and experiences. Jasmine's diverse background and skills, coupled with her adaptability and enthusiasm, make her a well-rounded individual ready to tackle challenges in both her studies and future career.
